What is Our Technology:

Team 8 chose ChatGPT as our target technology for our final project presentation.

Introduction of ChatGPT:

ChatGPT is a recently made technology that serves as a generative artificial chatbot for users to have their questions answered, find inspiration by providing a range of topics, and offer assistance to fixing any issues. It is powered by a company called OpenAI. ChatGPT is developed by the components of “large strings of numbers” also known as weights or parameters to execute accurate information or answers, as there is code made to utilize those numbers resulting in the accuracy of the information provided. The information derived to meet the needs of users’ requests is from the source of the public internet sites, but not limited to information that doesn’t involve hate speech, personal information, and other inappropriate content sources.

Importance of ChatGPT:

Our team chose to use ChatGPT as our target technology as it had become a transformative part of AI resolving into the daily lives of human production. ChatGPT offers a diverse range of versatile services such as conversing in human-like behaviors or assisting in daily tasks. With its speeding skill in meeting the demands of users, it provides great benefit in completing priorities in a timely manner and pulling off all the hassles away. However, it can also be taken advantage of for malicious purposes such as plagiarizing projects or creating unoriginal content which deteriorates human creativity.
